The Five Rules

04
  • Single-Family — Raleway only; hierarchy from weight and scale.
  • Two-Weight — 400 and 700, nothing between.
  • One-Accent — a single amber, ≤ 8% of any surface. No gradients.
  • Hairline-Is-The-Layer — 1px rules only; add whitespace, never weight.
  • Flat & Rectilinear — no shadows, no rounded corners, ever.
